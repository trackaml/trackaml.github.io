Machine Learning for Detecting Illicit Offshore Sand Mining Linked to Money Laundering and Corruption.

Save Earth

Our product solves the problem of detecting and combating illicit offshore sand mining, which poses significant environmental and economic risks. It addresses the challenge faced by regulatory authorities in identifying hidden activities that exploit coastal resources for illegal gains and facilitate money laundering and corruption.

Go to app

Problem Statement

Sand mining can have significant environmental and social impacts. Such as Habitat Destruction, Water Quality and Quantity, Ecosystem Disruption, Coastal Erosion,Social Impacts and Illegal Mining.

DATA SOURCE

Empower regulatory authorities and environmental agencies to take proactive measures in preserving coastal ecosystems and upholding transparency in resource management practices.

WHAT WE DO

Aims to leverage machine learning techniques to effectively detect instances of illicit offshore sand mining and identify any potential links to money laundering and corruption. By developing a robust and accurate system, we can assist regulatory authorities and environmental agencies in combating this significant issue, preserving coastal ecosystems, and ensuring transparency in resource management.

Data Collection and Integration

We will gather comprehensive and reliable datasets related to offshore sand mining, financial transactions, and corruption indicators. By integrating and preprocessing these diverse data sources, we will create a unified dataset for model development.

Feature Engineering

Our team will conduct in-depth analysis and exploration of the integrated dataset to identify relevant features. These features will capture the distinctive patterns and characteristics associated with illicit offshore sand mining, money laundering, and corruption. Careful consideration will be given to feature selection and engineering techniques to maximize the model's performance.

Model Development

We will design and train a machine learning model using advanced algorithms and techniques. The model will be trained on the integrated dataset, utilizing appropriate supervised learning methods to identify and classify instances of illicit offshore sand mining. The model will also incorporate anomaly detection techniques to flag suspicious activities related to money laundering and corruption.

Real-time Monitoring and Alert System

Our solution will implement a real-time monitoring system capable of processing large-scale data streams. By continuously analyzing incoming data, the system will promptly detect any potential instances of illicit offshore sand mining. When suspicious activities are identified, an alert system will be triggered, enabling authorities to take immediate action.

Interpretability and Transparency

We recognize the importance of interpretability and transparency in decision-making processes. Our solution will provide clear explanations for the model's detection outcomes, ensuring regulatory agencies and stakeholders understand the contributing factors. This transparency will foster trust and facilitate effective collaboration in combating illicit offshore sand mining.
